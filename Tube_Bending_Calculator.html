Bend Layout Calculator

Enter your bender radius, desired bend angle, and measurement to end of pipe. Calculates Setback, Advance, Holdback, and Gain — then walks you through the 5-step mark sequence.

5-Step Layout Procedure

This is the sequence you follow when marking tube for a bend. Each step builds on the previous mark.

The Formulas

Setback (SB)TAN(½ bend angle) × Radius of Bender
Advance (ADV)Radius of Bender × 0.01745 × Bend Angle
Holdback (HB)2 × Setback
Gain2 × (Setback − Advance)
Gain (alternate)(2 × Setback) − ADV
1EOP → ℄
Measure from End Of Pipe to the Centerline mark. This is your first measurement from the print.
2℄ → SB
From the centerline mark, measure the Setback distance and make a mark. This is where the bend begins.
3SB → ADV
From the setback mark, measure the Advance distance. This is the arc length of the bend.
4ADV → HB
From the advance mark, measure the Holdback distance. This positions the bender correctly.
5HB → New ℄ or EOP
From the holdback mark, measure to the next centerline or end of pipe for the next bend or cut.
Remember: Holdback = 2 × Setback. For a 15/16" bender doing a 90° bend, HB = SB + 9/16". For a 45° bend, HB = SB + 3/16".
